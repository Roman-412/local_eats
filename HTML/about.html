About Local Eats

Welcome to Local Eats, your favorite destination for finding the best local food shops and roadside favorites. We believe that the heart of every city is its street food and local vendors.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between hungry foodies and amazing local food creators who might not always have a big digital presence.

Help & FAQs

How do I track my order?
Once you place an order, you'll receive a confirmation. You can view your current orders in the "Track Order" section (Coming soon!).

Can I pay with cash?
Yes! We support UPI, Card, and Cash on Delivery.
